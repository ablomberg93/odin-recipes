Chicken Noodle Soup

This is a recipe for chicken noodle soup.

Description

Chicken noodle soup is a soup made from chicken, noodles, and vegetables. It is the perfect comfort food that is often served during the cold winter months.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken until cooked through.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it.
  3. Add the onion, carrots, and celery to the pot. Saute until tender
  4. Add the chicken broth and water.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add the egg noodles and cook until tender.
  7. Add the shredded chicken back into the pot.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot.
